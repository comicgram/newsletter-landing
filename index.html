What is it?

Comicgram is a monthly newsletter that goes straight to your inbox. The last friday of each month you will receive a new issue with information like:

  • A list of the main diferences between the comic and the movie.
  • A review of the movie and the comic.
  • Fun facts about both, the movie and the comic.
  • And much more.

It's for everyone

Doesn't matter if you don't read comic books or you don't know every single story of the characters; Comicgram is designed to please everyone, from the true comic readers to the ones that only watch the movies.
